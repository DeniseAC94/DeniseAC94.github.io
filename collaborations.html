Collaborations & Research Network.

My research and science communication work has developed through collaborations across universities, museums, research groups, and independent projects spanning geometric morphometrics, evolutionary biology, zoology, palaeontology, fish taxonomy, mammalian morphology, isotope ecology, zooarchaeology, and science communication.

Collaboration themes

Geometric morphometrics

Landmark-based studies of mammals, fishes, reptiles, fossils and anatomical structures.

Mammalian morphology

Collaborations involving Carnivora, bats, bears, canids, rats, and broader mammalian variation.

Fish taxonomy and morphology

Work on East African cichlids, Lake Malawi systems, Tanzanian riverine haplochromines, and taxonomic questions.

Fossils and deep time

Experience with fossil and palaeontological material, including morphology, zooarchaeology and deep-time questions.

Applied ecology and isotopes

Contributions to stable isotope, fisheries, ecological, and applied biological research.

Methods support

International tutorial support through YouTube and practical resources for shape analysis workflows.
